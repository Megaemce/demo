      PimpBot is a graphical interface, which let’s the user create a Nullsoft Installer (NSIS) for various Winamp plugins with just a few mouse-clicks.

      You are creating AVS, MilkDrop or SPS presets for Winamp and look for a convenient way to create installers. The PimpBot Compiler offers a graphical user interface for the creation of NSIS installers. Create installers that automatically detect Winamp, aswell as the APEs your presets might need. Customize the appearance of your installer by changing its icons or by applying any Winamp 5 Classic Skin. Install fonts, point users to the website. All this without any knowledge of NSIS, it just takes a couple of mouse-clicks.

      In addition, PimpBot contains a component for easy backups of Winamp related files like your AVS or MilkDrop presets and their required files. Unique to PimpBot Backup is it’s dual-backup funtionality, allowing two backup targets at once, with support for local directories, FTP servers, WebDAV and DropBox.

      visbot network has been founded in early 2003 by some remaining members of the avsociety. the aim was to establish a group of visualization artists, being organized like a record label. with time passing by, visbot has released over hundred visualization packs for winamp and invited young talents to join the group.

      the name of the group came up in summer 2002 at a laub concert in ghent, belgium.

      so far, visbot has published presets for winamp’s advanced visualization studio (avs) only. the avs plugin comes with all versions of winamp, except the lite version. it’s basically a toolkit of effects, highly customizable and extensible through addional plugins. a visualization can be saved as a file referred to as preset. video previews of presets by visbot are available on visbot television. if you need help setting up avs, here’s an extended guide.

      all downloads on this page are free, no ads or spyware are included. some packs are released under a creative commons license.
